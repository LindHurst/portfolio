Suburban Nature Video

The suburban nature themed video was created as a fun little artistic project. The inspiration, once again, was nature but for this video I wanted it to be showcased in a suburban setting. The video is supposed to help those realize that nature, even in a suburban setting, can be tranquil and calming. The video was created using free video/audio stock and was edited together using Adobe Premier Pro software.
