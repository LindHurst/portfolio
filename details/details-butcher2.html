Butcher Block (Mobile Development)

Butcher Block was my first mobile application prototype. It was designed to be an app that would pair whiskey and cuts of meat together was a delicious meal. It would also locate one of their brick and morter businesses near by to allow for the purchase of the cut of meat. The inspiration for this was just to create a fun, different type of app that could help people learn about pairings. This was a difficult process to create this simple app but it turned out looking pretty good.
